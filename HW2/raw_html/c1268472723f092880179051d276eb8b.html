Meet the Team

Meet the Team

Our Policy team is comprised by some of the nation's top civilian defense and military leaders, working hard for the American people every day.

Our Mission

To provide insightful and forward-thinking national security advice and support to the Secretary of Defense and Department of Defense leadership on matters affecting strategy, planning, management of international alliances and partnerships, crisis preparedness and response, budgetary and operational oversight, and representation of the Department in international engagements and interagency decision-making processes in service of U.S. national defense.
