To honor the legacy of Martin Luther King Jr., the UNC Hussman School of Journalism and Media will host a keynote address with Allen Buansi — a member of the North Carolina House of Representatives and civil rights lawyer.
Refreshments will be available at 5:30 p.m. prior to the event’s start. Buansi’s keynote address will begin at 6 p.m.
UNC Hussman Assistant Professor Carl Kenney and Director of Career Services Jay Eubank will moderate a discussion with Buansi following his address.
