In February's Carolina Science Cafe, we will be joined by Dr. Seun Oyetade from the Cahoon Group in the UNC Department of Chemistry to discuss his research on solar fuels.
Designed for adults, Carolina Science Cafe is a free program that explores science topics making national and international headlines and offers the chance to meet the experts behind the work.
Carolina Science Cafe is hosted at Haw River Tap & Table in Carrboro with light appetizers available for all attendees. Bring your friends!
